About Us

Who We Are

SafeGreen Adventure Therapy is a skilled team of social care workers that specialise in alternative therapy and trauma-informed care.

We create family-focused and goal-oriented experiences to meet the needs of vulnerable young people and promote long-term positive change.

Our Character

Every SafeGreen program is unique to each young person and family referred to us.

We create programs that combine one-to-one interventions, focused on building individual resilience, with family based interventions, focused on building trust, communication and family functioning.

Activities

Adventure therapy activities can include problem solving activities and initiatives such as hiking, orienteering, canoeing/kayaking, rock-climbing, challenge courses, and overnight camping.

Specific therapeutic and trauma-informed considerations are made for every activity that we plan for young people and families.

Where We Operate

Adenture Therapy operates throughout Ontario, and remotely throughout Canada

We are a publicly supported organisation, and work closely with statutory and voluntary organisations across Ontario. Our Remote Office also accepts referrals throughout Ontario and Canada.
